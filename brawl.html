Brawl Star info guide

Image character Description
Colt Colt is a rare brawler, who doesn't have lots of health but does have high dmaage. Everytime he uses his attack, he shoots 6 bullets. Colt's ultimate is the pretty much the same but with wall-breaking bullets and higher damage. So, if you're a sharpshooter and have good aim, this character is for you!
Bo Bo is a epic brawler, he's another marksman who has pretty good damage and moderate health. His attack is a bow that shoots 3 arrows. His ultimate is a arrow shot into the air, and releasing 3 mines into the ground, activated when someone steps on it. I don't like his attack due to inconsistant damage in a long-range battle. But his ult is pretty good damage wise and defense with the small knockback
Barley Next up we have Barley. Barley is a rare character. He's a splash so low health but good damage. His attack is hm throwing a bottle releasing a puddle of poison. His ultimate is Barley throwing 5 bottles of poison creating a larger puddle of poison. I like him personally as I like playing more passively and Barley is perfect for that, with small assists in duos.
Shelly Shelly is a common, but don't let that decieve you she's one of the better damage-dealing units in the mid and early ranks. Her weapon is a shotgun and as such the closer she is to her target, the more damage is dealt. Her ultimate is similar to her attack, except her ultimate has knockback and can destroy walls like Colt.
Rico Rico is a super rare brawler. He has a simiar attack to Colt but his bullets bounces of walls, so any trickshoters would love this character. Rico's does good damage, his ultimate shoots out more bouncy bullets.
Carl Carl is another Super Rare brawler who throws his pickaxe like a boomerang. He has high hp but moderate damage. He reloads faster if his attack hits a wall without reaching full distance. His ultimate is a close range attack, with him spinning in a frenzy doing continuous damage. Overall pretty good character, just not too good in close quarters unless he has ultimate ready.
